Our College

Best Engineering College in Pondicherry

Top 10 Engineering Colleges in Tamilnadu

Sri YASH Engineering College, Puducherry is the best Engineering College in Puducherry. The prime objective of this institute is to impart quality technical education to all the sectors of the society.

Sri YASH Engineering College, is recognized as the best engineering in India and top engineering college in Puducherry. The institute is flourishing with students achievements and placement record with its enormous human and infrastructure resources.

One among the top ten engineering colleges in Tamilnadu is Sri YASH Engineering College which is well known for its placement activities due to which students got placed in multinational companies. Students and their parents feel pride to get admission in Sri YASH Engineering College.

The engineering college in Puducherry is Sri YASH Engineering College. Students of Sri YASH Engineering College obtained gold medals and top ten ranks in Pondicherry University exams. Parents prefer their wards to be admitted in SMVEC

Sri YASH Engineering College is one of the best engineering colleges in Puducherry affiliated to Pondicherry University, Accredited by NAAC with A grade, Accredited by NBA AICTE, New Delhi, an ISO 9001:2000 certified institution and Accredited by TATA consultancy Services.

The best college in Pondicherry is SMVEC ranked by EDU RAND and one among the 100 private engineering colleges in India by Education World a human development magazine. SMVEC Puducherry is the best college in offering on campus recruitment. SMVEC provides more placement opportunity for the students.
